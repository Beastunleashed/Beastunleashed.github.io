Players 1-5

These guys are the best of the best.

Arguably the best cricketer of all time, Sachin Tendulkar has to be on the top 10. His flawless technique, and his drive to win was up there with the very best.

Sachin Tendulkar

for more info on Sachin Tendulkar click here
Don Bradman was the hero of Australia during the early to mid 1900s. His hunger for success, his steely determination, made him one of the best.

Don Bradman

Jack Hobbs was a genuine person at heart. No bad word was written about him. His mastery of batting inspired many, and made him one of cricket's greatest.

Jack Hobbs

You probably won't find a player as all rounded as Garfield Sobers. His batting average was up there with legendary batsmen. He could bowl two styles of spin, and was also an excellent fast-medium bowler.

Garry Sobers

The original power player, Vivian Richards played a major role in the success of the West Indies during the late 1900s. His physique, and sheer strength allowed him to batter many a bowler.

Viv Richards

Muttiah Muralitharan is the king of spin. He has taken 800 wickets in tests, a record that seems almost unbreakable. He is also an extremely honest and noble player.

Muttiah Muralitharan
